Screen Repair on an old Phone

05 Juli 2026

This Project is about a screen repair I done on a xiaomi note 7 by myself. I had two of these laying around at home, one was missing a power button and wouldn't turn on, and the other one had a broken screen. When my sister needed an emergency phone, I used my heatgun to losen the displays of both phones and switched them so that I would have a working phone for my sister.

Because I didn't have real waterproofing for the Phone I just closed it back up with the original seal. It held a while but today it sits in my drawer again. So if you are a company planning to hire me, you can ask to see the phone to inspect the work I done.

I'm also planning to build a small portable computer with this phone by attaching a keyboard and tackpad and 3D-Printing a case for it. As soon as I'm finished with that you will find the Files here or on my GitHub.
