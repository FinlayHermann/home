Senheiser Momentum 4 Review

06 Juli 2026

This is a Review for the Senheiser Momentum 4, which I bought a while ago.

The Positive

The Senheiser Momentum 4 are very good and very well engineered headphones, they are comfortable over long periods of time (I could even sleep with them and had no issues), have an amazing sound quality (I especially like the bass, which they perfected) and the biggest thing I like about them, that the have a phone app you DON'T need to sign in and still get the full benefits.

The Negative

There are only a few negative points about such well made headphones, but there still are some, starting with the connectivity, they don't support true lossless audio via bluetooth, which is not a big issue considering most people won't hear the diffrence, but they also have slight connectivity problems with some phones, and with mine, they recently started to peel of on the cushions of the ear covers.

Result

The Momentum 4 are great headphones, although they are expensive, they have an amazing quality and are well built, software and hardware wise, and they only have minor issues which get mostly overshadowed by the good sound quality they provide.
